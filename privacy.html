Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-09-04

QuickQuizz uses TikTok’s Content Posting API to publish pre-rendered trivia videos to the authenticated user’s account.

Data We Collect

  • OAuth tokens from TikTok (access/refresh) to publish on your behalf.

How We Use Data

  • Authenticate you and post videos you trigger from our tool.
  • No user uploads, no tracking, no ads, no sale of data.

Storage & Retention

  • Tokens are stored securely (environment variables or encrypted storage) and kept only as needed.

Sharing

  • No sharing with third parties except TikTok APIs to perform posting.

Your Choices

  • You can revoke access anytime in TikTok settings; we will no longer be able to post.
  • To request deletion of any stored tokens, contact us at platiquizz@gmail.com.
